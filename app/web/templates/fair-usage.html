Fair Usage

Converter Downloads

This page defines acceptable usage patterns for this application instance.

Allowed

  • Personal, lawful conversion of content you own or are authorized to process.
  • Reasonable usage volume that does not degrade service reliability.
  • Testing and educational use that respects copyright and platform terms.

Not Allowed

  • Copyright infringement or unauthorized redistribution.
  • Circumventing access controls, anti-bot measures, or technical protections.
  • Automated high-volume scraping, abuse, or denial-of-service behavior.
  • Use that violates applicable laws or third-party terms.

Operational Limits

  • Queueing, retries, and timeout settings may enforce practical limits.
  • The operator may restrict, suspend, or delete jobs that appear abusive.

Retention

Outputs and metadata may be retained locally for convenience unless manually removed. Users should manage local retention according to their needs and legal obligations.
